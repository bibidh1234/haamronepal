Welcome to Nagarkot

Nagarkot

Nagarkot is a scenic hill station located just a short drive from Kathmandu, renowned for its breathtaking views of the Himalayas. Sitting at an altitude of 2,195 meters, it offers panoramic views of the snow-capped peaks, including Mount Everest on clear days. Known for its tranquil atmosphere and cooler climate, it is a popular retreat from the city's hustle and bustle.

Visitors flock to Nagarkot for its stunning sunrise and sunset views, as the peaks are illuminated by the golden light. The area is also a great destination for nature lovers and hikers, with numerous trails leading through lush forests and charming local villages.

Whether you’re there to relax or embark on a short trek, Nagarkot offers a serene escape and one of the best spots to admire the majesty of the Himalayas.

Top Things to Do in Nagarkot

  • Watch the sunrise over the Himalayas from Nagarkot View Tower
  • Enjoy panoramic views of Mount Everest and other peaks on a clear day
  • Take a peaceful nature walk through the forests surrounding Nagarkot
  • Go for a short trek to nearby villages like Dhulikhel or Changu Narayan
  • Relax and enjoy the cool climate and scenic beauty of the hill station
  • Visit local cafes and enjoy Nepali tea with a view

Where to Stay

Nagarkot offers a variety of accommodation options, from budget guesthouses to more luxurious resorts. Some popular choices include:

  • Club Himalaya by ACE Hotels
  • Hotel at the End of the Universe
  • Niva Niwa Lodge
  • Peaceful Cottage & Cafe du Mont
  • Bhangeri Durbar Resort

Local Cuisine

While in Nagarkot, be sure to try some of the local dishes, many of which reflect the traditional Nepali food culture:

  • Dal Bhat: A traditional Nepali meal of lentil soup, rice, and vegetables
  • Momos: Nepal’s popular dumplings with meat or vegetable filling
  • Thukpa: A hearty noodle soup perfect for the cooler weather
  • Gundruk: A fermented leafy green vegetable, a staple in Nepali cuisine
  • Local tea: Sip on a warm cup of Nepali tea while enjoying the views
