Welcome to Bandipur

Bandipur

Bandipur is a charming hilltop town in Nepal that has preserved its traditional Newari culture and architecture. With its stunning panoramic views of the Himalayas, this town is a haven for those seeking both natural beauty and cultural richness. Explore the traditional architecture, enjoy the serene environment, and immerse yourself in local traditions.

Top Attractions in Bandipur

  • Enjoy panoramic views from the Siddha Cave, one of the largest caves in Nepal.
  • Stroll through the charming streets of Bandipur and admire the traditional Newari architecture.
  • Visit the historical Bandipur Bazaar and the beautiful Bindebasini Temple.
  • Take a hike through the surrounding hills for breathtaking scenery and tranquility.
  • Experience the local culture and festivals that highlight Bandipur’s rich heritage.

Where to Stay

Bandipur offers a variety of accommodation options that blend traditional charm with modern comfort. Here are some popular choices:

  • Bandipur Mountain Resort - Offers stunning views and comfortable rooms with traditional décor.
  • Old Inn Bandipur - A charming guesthouse with a warm atmosphere and traditional Newari architecture.
  • Gaun Ghar - Provides a rustic experience with beautiful views of the surrounding hills.
  • Himalayan Lodge - A cozy lodge offering a peaceful retreat with modern amenities.

Local Cuisine

Bandipur’s food scene offers a mix of traditional Newari dishes and local specialties. Be sure to try these local delicacies:

  • Dal Bhat: A traditional meal of lentil soup, rice, and vegetables.
  • Momos: Dumplings with various fillings, a popular snack in Nepal.
  • Chatamari: A traditional Newari dish, often referred to as a "Nepali pizza."
  • Sel Roti: A traditional Nepali rice doughnut.
  • Local tea and coffee from the town’s quaint cafés.
