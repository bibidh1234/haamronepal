Things to Do

Nepal offers a wide range of activities to suit every adventurer and traveler. Here are some top activities you shouldn't miss:

Trekking

Embark on breathtaking treks like the Everest Base Camp or Annapurna Circuit to experience Nepal's stunning landscapes and rich culture.

Wildlife Safari

Explore the national parks of Chitwan and Bardia for exciting wildlife safaris. Spot tigers, rhinos, and elephants in their natural habitats.

Cultural Tours

Discover the rich heritage of Nepal with visits to ancient temples, palaces, and traditional villages.

Adventure Sports

Try thrilling adventure sports like paragliding in Pokhara or white-water rafting in the Himalayan rivers.

Local Cuisine

Nepal's culinary landscape is diverse and rich. Here are some must-try dishes:

Momo

Momos are delicious dumplings filled with meat or vegetables and served with a spicy dipping sauce.

Dal Bhat

This traditional meal consists of steamed rice, lentil soup, and various accompaniments like vegetables and pickles.

Gundruk

A fermented leafy green vegetable often served as a side dish, Gundruk has a tangy flavor.

Newari Cuisine

Enjoy traditional Newari dishes like Bara, Yomari, and spicy meat dishes.

Sel Roti

A traditional rice-based doughnut that is crispy on the outside and soft inside, often enjoyed during festivals.

Thukpa

A flavorful noodle soup with meat or vegetables, perfect for cold days.
